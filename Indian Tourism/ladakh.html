About Ladakh

Ladakh, located in the northernmost region of India, is known for its barren yet beautiful landscapes, stunning monasteries, and adventure tourism. Surrounded by the majestic Himalayas and Karakoram ranges, it is a dream destination for nature lovers, trekkers, and those seeking spiritual solace.

Top Attractions

Pangong Lake

A mesmerizing high-altitude lake that changes colors, famous for its beauty and serenity.

Leh Palace

A majestic 17th-century palace offering panoramic views of the Leh town and the Himalayas.

Magnetic Hill

A unique phenomenon where vehicles appear to defy gravity and move uphill on their own.
